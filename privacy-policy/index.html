Privacy Policy

Your trust is important to us. Learn how we handle your data.

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

At OrthoGuide, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our clinic-based orthodontic system.

1. Information We Collect

OrthoGuide is a managed clinical system. Since there is no public signup, all user accounts are created and managed by authorized administrative personnel.

  • Professional Information: Names and roles of clinical staff.
  • Patient Information: Patient IDs, full names, clinical data, treatment progress, and orthodontic records.
  • Usage Logs: System logs to ensure security and audit trails for clinical compliance.

2. How Information is Used

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Providing clinical tools for orthodontic management.
  • Tracking patient treatment progress and appointment scheduling.
  • System maintenance and security audits.

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect sensitive clinical data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Your Rights

Patients and staff can request access to their records, data correction, or account deletion by contacting their respective clinic administrator or follows the procedures in our support guidelines.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at support@orthoguide.com.
